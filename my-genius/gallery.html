Famous Golf Photos

The first golf course was created in Scotland in the 16th century, and the first recorded tournament, the Silver Club competition, was held in 1744. From there, the game continued to grow in popularity, with the first golf club, The Royal and Ancient Golf Club of St Andrews, being established in 1754. In the 1800s, golf made its way to the United States and became popular among the wealthy elite. The first American golf club, the Saint Andrew's Golf Club, was established in 1888 in New York. Today, golf is played all over the world and is enjoyed by millions of people.
