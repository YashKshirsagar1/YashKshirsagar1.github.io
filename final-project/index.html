How We Started

Founded in August 2010, the School for the Blind at Vivek Education Foundation (VEF) provides training and education, free of cost, to children and adults who are visually impaired so they can acquire communication skills, and become socially self-sufficient and independent citizens of the community. The School also assists children with their academic coursework towards completion of their grades. VEF started in 2005 as a small Non-Government Agency in the home of Mr. and Mrs. Vivek Shinde of Mankhurd, a suburb of Mumbai, with the primary goal of providing educational support for children from the neighboring slums.


The Shindes used personal funds to purchase supplies and books for these children as well as make contributions towards their mainstream school tuition fees. Very soon, their passion expanded into helping divorced and widowed women who needed financial support and resources to pursue educational programs or start small businesses.
